Make a perfect website
with the ultimate social features to fit your needs

Ning is the largest social website platform, which makes it easy to build your online identity and grow a successful, thriving network around.

Start your social website
from a scratch in a matter of minutes

Ning’s social platform provides the powerful tools you’ll need to create, control & monetize your own social network.

Drag & drop site builder

Creating a social website from scratch is a breeze with our drag and drop site builder – no coding experience needed! Simply select a layout for your page, then drag and drop content modules where you want them. With layout options available for each page, you can place content just so, creating a different look for each page on your network.

Broad customization

Choose from a number of ready-made themes in the Design Studio to get started, using the settings to personalize your network's appearance. Know how to code? Add your own HTML or CSS to further customize the look and feel of your network in the Advanced section

Custom domains

Already have a domain you want to use? We've got you covered! Use up to three custom domains to further brand and customize your network. Moreover, you can purchase your domains anywhere and map them to your network in three simple steps!

Speedy hosting

We’ve got your back! Using industry standard security measures to keep your content safe, we're able to maintain an uptime rate of 99.9% to keep your network online and speedy.
We host networks that range from a few hundred members to over a million, so we'll be with you every step of the way as your network grows. Just build it, we’ll host it!

Social integration

Make Ning your one-stop shop for everything social for your community. The Social Sign In simplifies and speeds up the sign up flow, allowing new members to use their existing credentials from Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google, and more. Pull in content from Facebook, Twitter, and RSS feeds into your Activity Feed never been easier.

24/7 Customer service

Have a question about setting things up? Something doesn't look right? Can’t find the answer in our online Help Center? With 24/7 customer service you will easily solve any issues, just call or email us whenever you need.

Your content, your brand, your control

You’ve built an online presence for your brand, now take to the next level and create an online home for your brand with Ning. No paid promotions - your members see exactly what you want them to and when you want them to see it. Use Broadcast Messages to share highlighted content directly to your members. Your members, your content, and your brand, the way you want it!

Become visible and grow
your social network

Ning has everything you need to build your social website: easy in use drag and drop site builder, custom domains, customizable themes, SEO, responsive design and 24/7 support. All the most powerful features to create your network are already inside - Blogs, Forums, Groups, Events, Photos, and many more!

Turn your Ning Network to perfection

Create your own website and grow a thriving community around it using Ning’s preinstalled features and functions: secure hosting, SEO tools, beautiful templates and themes. No hidden charges! We'll be with you every step of the way with 24/7 customer support.

Why wait?
Build your website today following these easy steps!

1. Sign up for free, select the perfect subdomain for your network and get free 14-days trial. No charges!
2. Choose the template that best fits your community and customize your network's appearance with the Design Studio.
3. Add some content and Get the word out!
